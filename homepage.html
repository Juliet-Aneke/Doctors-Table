Lifecare Health center is a local clinic. It treats people's medical, dental, mental health, substance use, and other health care needs.The Health Center is community-based and led in part by patients.It offers services ranging from general consultations to specialized care (neurosurgery, cardiology) and digital health.

Lifecare Health Center was founded in 2012 to address the gap between primary care and emergency rooms, offering immediate care with extended hours and no appointments. Starting with one location in downtown, the Health Center rapidly expanded to three locations by 2018, integrating onsite diagnostic imaging and laboratory services. In 2020, Lifecare Health Center pivoted to include telemedicine options, focusing on convenience, rapid patient access, and digital-first health management to serve busy urban professionals and families.
